To My Forever, My Azhagi ❤️

If someone asked me what love looks like, I wouldn’t describe it with words—I would simply tell them to look at you.

You are the kind of man people write stories about but rarely get to meet in real life. A man whose strength isn’t measured by how loudly he speaks, but by how gently he loves. A man whose heart is so pure that everyone around him feels safe, respected, and valued. You have this beautiful way of making life feel lighter without even trying.

Before you came into my life, I believed love was just a feeling. But you taught me that love is much more than butterflies or excitement. Love is choosing each other every single day. Love is standing beside one another when life isn’t easy. Love is the comfort of knowing that no matter what happens, there is one person who will never let go of your hand.

That person is you.

You are the first thought that makes me smile in the morning and the last prayer I whisper before I sleep. Even on the busiest days, somehow my heart finds its way back to you. Home has never been a place for me anymore. Home is wherever you are.

There are moments when I secretly watch you while you’re busy doing something, and I wonder, “How did I get someone this wonderful?” Your smile has a way of calming every storm inside me. Your voice feels like peace after a long day. Even your silence speaks a language that only my heart understands.

You don’t have to be perfect to be extraordinary. It’s your little imperfections, your genuine heart, your patience, your honesty, and your quiet care that make you the most beautiful person I’ve ever known. You don’t try to impress anyone, yet you impress me every single day simply by being yourself.

Thank you for believing in me even when I struggle to believe in myself. Thank you for celebrating my happiest moments as though they were your own and for standing beside me through the difficult ones without ever making me feel alone. Thank you for loving me in ways I never knew I needed.

Sometimes I wonder if you truly realize how special you are. You are someone’s answered prayer. You are someone’s greatest blessing.

You are mine.

I promise that no matter how many years pass, no matter how much our hair turns grey or our faces change with time, I will still look at you with the same admiration I do today. I will still reach for your hand in every crowded place. I will still laugh at your jokes, annoy you for attention, steal your hoodies, and find comfort in your hugs.

I don’t want a perfect life.

I just want a lifetime with you.

I want to celebrate your victories, wipe your tears when life gets difficult, support your dreams, grow old beside you, and someday sit with you on our balcony, holding your hand, smiling at the beautiful life we built together.

If there is another life after this one, I hope destiny is kind enough to let our paths cross again. And if I have the privilege of choosing once more, my answer will never change.

I will choose you.
In every lifetime.
In every universe.
In every version of my story.

Because loving you has been the easiest, most beautiful decision my heart has ever made.

You are my best friend.
You are my greatest blessing.
You are my peace.
You are my forever.

And if there is one thing I want you to remember every single day, it is this:

No matter where life takes us, no matter what challenges come our way, there will always be one heart that beats a little faster just because you exist.

That heart is mine.

Forever and always,
Monica ❤️
